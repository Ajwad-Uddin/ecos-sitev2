Why ECOS

What Sets Us Apart

We do things the right way—not the cheap way. Here's what that means for your organization.

Our Trucks, Our People

No subcontractors, no third-party haulers. Our uniformed, background-checked employees show up in company trucks. Complete chain of custody from your door to destruction.

100% Domestic Processing

Your equipment never leaves US soil. We don't ship overseas where regulations are lax and data security is questionable. Everything happens right here in Georgia.

Value Recovery, Not Disposal Fees

Most ITAD vendors charge you to take equipment that has resale value. We flip that model—qualifying equipment earns you revenue, not a bill. Transparent pricing, always.

Audit-Ready Documentation

Every asset gets serial-number-level tracking. You receive comprehensive Certificate of Destruction, chain of custody records, and recycling reports that satisfy any compliance audit.

Zero Landfill Commitment

Nothing goes to the dump. Equipment is refurbished for reuse, parts are harvested for secondary markets, and remaining materials are responsibly recycled. Sustainability isn't a buzzword—it's our process.

NIST 800-88 Data Destruction

We don't just delete files—we destroy data using methods that meet federal standards. Degaussing, overwriting, and physical destruction options based on your security requirements.
