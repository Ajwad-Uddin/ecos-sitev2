Common Questions

Is pickup really free?

Yes, for qualifying equipment volumes (typically 20+ units). For smaller quantities, we offer affordable pickup options or you can drop off at our Tucker, GA facility.

How is data destroyed?

We use NIST 800-88 compliant methods including DoD-level data wiping and physical destruction (shredding). You choose the method based on your security requirements.

What documentation do I receive?

You receive a serialized Certificate of Destruction listing every asset by serial number, plus chain of custody documentation and recycling reports.

Do you handle compliance requirements?

Yes. We're experienced with HIPAA, SOX, GLBA, FERPA, and NIST requirements. We can sign Business Associate Agreements and provide all required documentation.

What if my equipment has no resale value?

We still handle it responsibly with our zero-landfill commitment. Mixed loads with valuable equipment often offset disposal costs entirely.

How long does the process take?

Quote within 24 hours. Pickup scheduled at your convenience (often within 1-2 weeks). Certificate of Destruction typically delivered within 5-7 business days of pickup.
